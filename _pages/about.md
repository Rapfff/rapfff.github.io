About me
--------
I am a post-doctoral researcher at [eawag](https://www.eawag.ch/en/), the Swiss Federal Institute of Aquatic Science and Technology.
I develop, design and implement **machine learning** methods applied on **hydrological systems**.

I completed my PhD at Reykjavik University (Iceland), focusing on **machine learning** techniques for **Markov model** learning, under the supervision of Anna Ingolfsdottir and [Giovanni Bacci](https://homes.cs.aau.dk/~giovbacci/).

About my main project
---------------------
At eawag, our project objective is to integrate **deep learning** methodologies with traditional **process-based** approaches to develop a **hybrid model for hydrological research**. Essentially, we are constructing deep neural networks wherein the neurons are replaced with reservoirs.

Research interests
------------------
Machine learning, hydrology, model checking, markov models, statistical modeling.
